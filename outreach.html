Outreach

I am a coordinator and founding member of POWER Bay Area, which is a mentoring program and workshop series. We aim to support women and students from other marginalised genders and backgrounds, and welcome participation from anyone who feels they would benefit. Additional information is available on the POWER Bay Area website

Letters to a Pre-Scientist

Letters to a Pre-Scientist connects students to STEM professionals to demystify STEM careers and to inspire all students to explore a future in STEM.
